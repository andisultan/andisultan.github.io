At Evergreen Sourcing, We Don’t Just Source Good Quality Products At Exceptionally Good Prices, We Deliver Value!

We do so by leveraging our years of sourcing experience in Asia combined with international perspective on product quality and trends. Our customized sourcing solutions cater to businesses of different scale and requirement.

Effective Sourcing

Our priority is to enable our global partners establish an extension of their business in Asia, backed with a solid team of professionals who can effectively manage their procurement efforts through clear understanding of their core products. Given our limited product scope, we possess deeper knowledge of quality benchmark, technical features, applications, import requirements, packaging, trends and value of each item. This focused sourcing strategy will ensure that you get the right products at the right price, all the time.

Diversify Your Supply Source

With our network of offices in major manufacturing hubs in Asia, we can diversify your sourcing options for good value products that will enhance your competitive edge.

Flexibility

Our sourcing infrastructure is designed to facilitate importers of different size and requirements. We can customize our sourcing solutions to help small-to-medium sized retailers looking to expand their product range or large-scale/chain retailers looking to enhance their competitive strength and control by localizing supply chain management in Asia.

Professional & Ethical

We understand the challenges of international trade; therefore as an Asian company with a global business perspective, we are strategically positioned to bridge the cultural divide by operating with local awareness and sensibility coupled with international business ethics and professionalism. Our multinational and multilingual sourcing specialists are fluent in English, Chinese, Spanish and Bahasa Indonesia.

Proactive Research

Our sourcing specialists regularly attend trade shows all over Asia in search for the latest products and innovations in building supplies, furniture, home improvement and green product categories. As our client, you will receive new product catalogs, newsletters and special offers on a regular basis so you can make informed buying decisions.

Environmentally Conscious

We are mindful of the environment when selecting the right product, manufacturer, and packaging options. While it is not always easy to find the right balance between value and sustainability, we aspire to keep learning and developing ways to contribute for a better planet.

Strong Relationships

Our company representatives make frequent visits to our clients around the world as a way to forge stronger relationships and to better understand changing market demands.

Sourcing Solutions

We offer specially formulated procurement services that caters to small and medium sized re-sellers which are suitable for hardware stores, supermarkets, and general variety stores that are looking to reduce their inventory cost and speed up their product turnover.

For larger retailers looking to enhance their competitive strength and control by localizing supply chain management in Asia, we offer a comprehensive solution that covers; Product Sourcing & Development, Factory Audit, OEM Services, Branding, Third-Party Testing & Certification and Shelf-Ready Packaging Solutions (SRP).

We are qualified supplier for open tender bids in Asia and South America.
