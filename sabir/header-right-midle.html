About us

Making the world a better place by creating a
revolutionary kamera app that is SoLoMo.

We at Kamera want to revolutionize the way you take pictures. Taking pictures shouldn’t be complicated, it should be seamless. We are different because our technology is truly SoLoMo.

How did we come up with Kamera? We’ll, we were all at a house party. And Bill G showed up with his crew, and we had far too many to drink that night. So, we get our *smart*phone out trying to take a picture with a legend, and it just wouldn’t work. When we did finally manage to take a picture. We wanted to hide few things from it (drunken nerds are not pleasant to look at) and put some filters on. Let’s just say the default camera on the phone sucked, and all the apps we downloaded were really not user-friendly i.e. lame.

As the morning sun started to rise, we realised something needed to be done. We wanted to make the world a better place by creating a superior camera app that would let people take, edit and share pictures easily than ever before.

oin our revolution if you believe a picture is worth a thousand word. No picture should ever be unfiltered or unshared. No selfies should ever look like you just vomited.

We are not stopping at just taking pictures. We are creating a new way to capture motion videos, edit them and sharing it with your friends and family. Because people deserve a truly revolutionary photography and motion capturing app.
